My Language Buddy




Is a project focused on teaching languages, in a specialized and personalized way, focused on small groups of students, who can choose

the time, place and content of the courses in order to provide an international learning experience creating a link between the

teacher and students to accelerate learning and ensure the development thereof.

Food
Transportation
