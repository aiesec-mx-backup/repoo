My Mexican Experience

What customer say about us and why love Mexico!

  • In an AIESEC IGCDP project I spent six weeks in Mexico where my work was teaching English to young people. At the beginning I was a bit afraid of going to Mexico, which is so far and different from Hungary but now I consider this idea as one of my best decision in my life. I was absolutely satisfied with the AIESECers in Mexico: they never let us go alone or get lost and always helped us. Every weekend we were traveling to different parts of Mexico: Acapulco, Cancun, Puebla, Tlaxcala and a lot of times we spent the afternoons and nights in the center or with parties. On the whole I had a so fantastic time in Mexico and I can’t wait to go back!



    Georgina Szabó, Hungary My Language Buddy

  • My initial dream was so small in comparison what I have achieved during my internship in Mexico in June-August 2013 (Voyage Summer Camp in AIESEC México). Living life to the full, taking a risk, being part of a common goal, going through the stereotypes… Mexican hospitality is always in my heart. We were amazed by a virgin nature in Mexico climbing on top of volcanoes, seeing “wild jungles” like in movies. Each person in my team was unique. I met my belonged friends and I realized that there are no borders among countries and “it is all about people”. Teaching my students, supporting each other, we were parts of a big puzzle creating amazing picture, which is called “unique experience”. As a result, It makes you a real citizen of the world and you are ready to be a change.

    Maria Kuts, Ukraine Impacting Summer Camps

  • My name is Vinicius and in the last winter i decided to realice a practice in the city of Xalapa in Veracruz to teach portuguese to childrens. I never imagined that an experience in Mexico was so amazing. The cultural impact is large, the Mexican culture is very different from the Brazilian, but that's what made my exchange so special. Every day I had opportunities to meet people, food, places or just a new word in Spanish. The opportunity to teach my language, my culture and my present foreign country are things that are priceless, an experience that only those who live know to value. Thanks Aiesec México for this Wanderful experience



    Vinicius Weide Rodrigues, Brasil Sharing Languages

  • I'm super satisfied with my internship in PwC CMAAS, I'm learning a great deal of new things on daily basis, improving my technical skills, learning to take responsibilities, building network of contacts etc. The team accepted me as one of them. I have a excellent working atmosphere and a great deal of support in every aspect.

    Matea Curkovic
    Slavonski Brod, CROATIA     PWC Mexico

  • Currently, I am working in a company called Tech Innovations and I'm really enjoying my internship experience through AIESEC. I am working on a project to export tequila drinks to Japan. I am in constant communication with Canadian companies with whom we have alliances, laboratories, etc.. Before I was in management quality system and studied all ISO 9001 from start to finish and in Spanish. To be honest I am very happy with my Spanish, has finally improved (after 4 years of study at my university). My boss wants us to learn a lot and lets us exploit the professional world. The first thing I said was "I want to be open" and that's what he's done. So I'm very grateful to the Mexicans, I am extremely happy to be here until February. México is amazing!

    Valeria Kachmar, Russia Tech Innovations
